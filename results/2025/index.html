Results 2025

The 2025 edition of the Second Infodengue-Mosqlimate Dengue Challenge (2nd IMDC Sprint) concluded with a final webinar held on October 31, 2025. This highly successful edition demonstrated global collaboration, featuring:

  • 15 teams from various international locations.
  • A contribution of 19 distinct predictive models for dengue forecasting.

2. Ensemble Model Analysis

The project utilized an ensemble model (a combination of top-performing models) to create a potentially more robust and accurate forecast.

  • Ensemble Documentation: The methodology and specific performance results of the ensemble are documented here: https://github.com/Mosqlimate-project/2nd_IMDC_sprint_results/blob/main/ensemble_results.md
  • Ensemble Presentation: The dedicated video presentation summarizing the ensemble results is available here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sb3lv3hGwGg
