The project utilized an **ensemble model** (a combination of top-performing models) to create a potentially more robust and accurate forecast.

* **Ensemble Documentation:** The methodology and specific performance results of the ensemble are documented in this paper: [Leveraging probabilistic forecasts for dengue preparedness and control: the 2024 Dengue Forecasting Sprint in Brazil](https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.05.12.25327419v1).
