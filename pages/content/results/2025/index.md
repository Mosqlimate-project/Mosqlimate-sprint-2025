The **2025 edition** of the **Second Infodengue-Mosqlimate Dengue Challenge (2nd IMDC Sprint)** concluded with a final webinar held on **October 31, 2025**. This highly successful edition demonstrated global collaboration, featuring:

* **15 teams** from various international locations.
* A contribution of **19 distinct predictive models** for dengue forecasting.
